Getting Started

What is Retire Early?

Retire Early is a financial planning app designed to help you achieve Financial Independence and Retire Early (FIRE). It helps you calculate your FIRE number, track your progress, and plan for major life events.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple:

  • Download the app from the App Store or Google Play
  • Create an account or continue as a guest
  • Enter your basic financial information (income, expenses, savings)
  • See your personalized FIRE number and timeline
Is my financial data secure?

Yes, your data security is our top priority. All financial data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted end-to-end. We never sell or share your personal financial information.

FIRE Calculations

How is my FIRE number calculated?

Your FIRE number is calculated using the 4% rule: Annual Expenses x 25. This gives you the portfolio size needed to safely withdraw 4% per year to cover your expenses indefinitely.

For example, if your annual expenses are £36,000, your FIRE number would be £900,000.

What is the 4% rule?

The 4% rule comes from the Trinity Study, which found that withdrawing 4% of your portfolio annually gives you a 95% chance of your money lasting 30+ years. It's a widely used guideline in the FIRE community for retirement planning.

What return rate does the app assume?

By default, the app uses a 7% annual return rate, which is the historical average for a diversified stock portfolio after inflation. You can customize this rate in the calculator settings to match your investment strategy.

What are the different FIRE types?

There are four main FIRE types:

  • Lean FIRE: Retire with minimal expenses (£25k-£40k/year)
  • Barista FIRE: Semi-retirement with part-time work
  • Coast FIRE: Saved enough that compound growth covers retirement
  • Fat FIRE: Retire with a luxurious lifestyle (£100k+/year)

Features

What is Scenario Planning?

Scenario Planning lets you test "what-if" situations to see how they affect your FIRE timeline. You can add events like buying a house, having children, changing careers, or taking a sabbatical to see the impact on your financial independence date.

Can I track multiple scenarios?

Yes, you can create and save multiple scenarios to compare different life paths. This helps you make informed decisions about major financial choices.

How does the Compounding Breakdown work?

The Compounding Breakdown shows you year-by-year projections of your portfolio growth. You can see how your initial investment, monthly contributions, and compound returns combine to reach your FIRE goal.

Account & Subscription

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the core features of Retire Early are free. This includes the FIRE calculator, basic scenario planning, and the Learn section. Premium features like advanced analytics and unlimited scenarios are available with a subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's app store:

  • iOS: Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Retire Early > Cancel
  • Android: Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > Retire Early > Cancel
How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app: Profile > Account Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. Note that data stored locally on your device will need to be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't syncing my data

If your data isn't syncing, try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're signed in to your account
  • Pull down to refresh on the main screen
  • Sign out and sign back in
  • If the issue persists, contact support
I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.
