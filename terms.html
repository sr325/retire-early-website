Description of Service

MY FIRE JOURNEY is a personal finance planning and educational tool designed to help users explore the principles of Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE). The App provides the following features:

  • Milestones — Set and track progress towards personalised financial goals and milestones on your FIRE journey.
