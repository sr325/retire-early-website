Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

At Retire Early, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Retire Early is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Financial Data You Enter: Income, expenses, savings, and portfolio information you manually input into the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account for cloud sync, we collect your email address and encrypted authentication data.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app (no personal financial data is included).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • All financial calculations happen locally on your device
  • Your financial data is never sold or shared with third parties
  • Account data is used solely for authentication and cloud sync features
  • Anonymous analytics help us improve app performance and features

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: By default, all your financial data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers (powered by Supabase). You can delete your cloud data at any time from within the app.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for cloud-synced data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • No storage of sensitive credentials

5. Third-Party Services

FIRE App may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For optional cloud storage and authentication
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking (no personal data)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about you
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

7. Children's Privacy

FIRE App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email if you have an account.

9. Contact Us
