Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026  ·  Governed by the laws of England and Wales
Important Disclaimer

MY FIRE JOURNEY is provided for educational and informational purposes only. The app, its content, tools, calculators, and any output they produce do not constitute professional financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. We are not qualified financial advisors, and nothing within the app should be interpreted as a personal recommendation. Please consult a qualified, FCA-regulated financial adviser before making any investment or financial planning decisions.

At MY FIRE JOURNEY ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data transparently and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what rights you have under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Section 01

Information We Collect

When you use MY FIRE JOURNEY, we collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Information — Your email address, first name, and last name, collected at signup.
  • Financial Data — Income, expenses, savings, portfolio values, and date of birth that you manually enter into the app.
  • FIRE Quiz Data — Your answers to the FIRE readiness quiz and the results generated from them.
  • Scenarios & Milestones — Custom financial scenarios and milestones you create within the app.
  • Daily Expense Tracking — Individual expense entries you log through the daily tracker feature.
  • Subscription & Payment Status — Whether you hold a free or premium subscription and the current status of your payment.
  • Billing Information — Billing address provided during the payment process (collected via Stripe and Google Places API).
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes under the lawful bases permitted by the UK GDPR:

  • Providing the Service — To operate the app, perform FIRE calculations, display your dashboard, track expenses, and deliver the features you use. (Lawful basis: contract performance)
  • Authentication — To verify your identity when you sign in via email and password, and to facilitate password resets via email link. (Lawful basis: contract performance)
  • Payment Processing — To process premium subscription payments through Stripe. We never store your card number or full payment details. (Lawful basis: contract performance)
  • Service Improvement — To understand how the app is used and to improve its features and performance. (Lawful basis: legitimate interest)
  • Communication — To notify you of important changes to the service, this policy, or your account. (Lawful basis: legitimate interest)

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Section 03

Data Storage & Security

All user data — including account information, financial data, quiz results, scenarios, milestones, and expense tracking entries — is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database. Supabase infrastructure is hosted on cloud servers which may be located outside the United Kingdom. Where data is transferred outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

Card and payment details are handled entirely by Stripe. MY FIRE JOURNEY never receives, processes, or stores your full card number, CVV, or sensitive payment credentials.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure authentication protocols, and access controls to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Section 04

Authentication

MY FIRE JOURNEY uses email and password authentication only. We do not offer social login (e.g. Google, Apple, Facebook). Your password is securely hashed and stored by Supabase — we never have access to your plaintext password. If you forget your password, you can request a reset link sent to your registered email address.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service providers to operate MY FIRE JOURNEY. Each processes data only as necessary to fulfil their specific function:

Provider Purpose Data Processed
Supabase Authentication, database storage, edge functions Account data, financial data, quiz results, scenarios, milestones, expense entries
Stripe Payment processing for premium subscriptions Card details, billing address, subscription status
Google Places API Address autocomplete during payment Billing address input

For more information, please refer to the privacy policies of Supabase, Stripe, and Google.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, all associated personal data — including financial data, quiz results, scenarios, milestones, and expense entries — will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records (e.g. transaction records for tax or regulatory purposes).

Section 07

Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Rectification Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
Data Portability Export your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
Restriction Request that we limit how we process your data.
Objection Object to processing based on our legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@myfirejourney.io. We will respond to your request within one calendar month. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

MY FIRE JOURNEY is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without appropriate parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at contact@myfirejourney.io.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email to your registered address. We encourage you to review this page periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was most recently revised.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch.
